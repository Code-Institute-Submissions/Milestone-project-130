Who are we?

Over 20 years of experience, MD Networks has become one of Irelands leading IT service providers. Centralisation of IT support & services is at the core of MD Networks strategy. We support and sell software and systems hardware and a broad range of ifrastructure, cloud, web development and consulting services. Highly qualified engineers and designers bring many years of experience and expertise from some of the most prestigious IT and Web Design companies in the world. Our goal is to provide you, our customers with friendly, informative and helpful IT support and services. We will keep you up-to-date and informed as we source, build and install suitable IT solutions which meet your exact needs and requirements. We offer non-contract interim or ad-hoc services as well as contract service plans providing the highest level of customer support with the widest choice of competitively priced products & services.
